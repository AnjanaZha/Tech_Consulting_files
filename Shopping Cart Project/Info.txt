Create a shopping cart with the following basic functionalities:
Display a menu of items with the corresponding quantities available in the store and the cost of the items.
Accept customer input for the items required.
Display a message if the item is out of stock.
Take input of customer details.
Calculate delivery charges based on the distance from the store.
Display a final bill with the items purchased and the customer details.
Conditions For delivery charges
    If upto 15 km then 50 Rs
    If 15 to 30 km then 100 rs
    else
    No delivery available
